Frequently Asked Questions

How can I report a listing?

Open the listing details screen for the ride, parcel, or service you want to report, then tap “Report this listing”. This will open your email app with a pre-filled report message.

How can I delete my account?

Open the Profile screen in the app and use the “Delete Account” option. The app will guide you through the confirmation steps.

How can I send feedback?

In the app, go to Profile → Help & Feedback and choose the feedback option, or email us directly using the support address above.
